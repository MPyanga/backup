THE CREATION OF HANGIFY

Hangify is a project developed in 2025 by a group of BSIT students from FEU Institute of Technology, utilizing its knowledge in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a fully functional website. By maximizing the potential of these technologies, the team integrated an API for database management, enabling seamless event creation, user interaction, and real-time data processing.

To ensure efficient development and collaboration, the team utilized GitHub repositories and work collaboration tools, allowing to effectively manage version control, share code, and combine their ideas. This collaborative approach enabled them to build a platform that brings their vision to life.

Hangify was inspired by the idea of connecting people and making it easier for individuals to share meaningful experiences. Whether it's discovering local concerts, planning get-togethers, or connecting with like-minded individuals, the platform aims to turn every event into a memorable experience.

Mission

Our mission is to connect people and communities by making it easier to discover exciting events, organize gatherings, and meet individuals who share the same interests. As a project developed by BSIT students of FEU Institute of Technology, we aim to transform ordinary moments into unforgettable experiences through seamless event discovery and community engagement.

Vision

Our vision is to become a platform that inspires meaningful connections and fosters vibrant communities. We aspire to make every event an opportunity for people to bond, share experiences, and create lasting memories, ultimately making social interactions more accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
